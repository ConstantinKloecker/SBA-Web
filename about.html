The story behind Storybook Adventures.

Built around a simple belief: the best way to introduce a child to creativity, technology, and AI is by starting with something every child already knows. A piece of paper and a pencil.

Built in Berlin For ages 6–12 GDPR & COPPA by design

From the founder

Hi, I'm Constantin. I built Storybook Adventures because I was tired of two things at the same time: kids spending hours scrolling through algorithm-curated content, and parents being afraid that AI would take creativity away from their children.

Both concerns are real. But I saw a third path. What if AI wasn't the thing that made the art, but the thing that brought the child's art to life? What if a creative app didn't reward endless scrolling, but rewarded sitting at the kitchen table with a pencil?

That's what I built. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Just real drawings, real children, and a little bit of magic at the end.

— Constantin Klöcker, Founder

What I believe

🔒

Privacy isn't a feature, it's the foundation.

Children never share their name, email, or any personal information. No account to create. An anonymous device identifier, nothing more. GDPR and COPPA by design, not retrofit.

🎨

Real creativity starts on real paper.

Most digital drawing apps replace pencil and paper. I built the opposite: most of the creative time happens off-screen, on real paper, with real tools. The phone is just the camera, the cinema, and a gentle guide.

📝

AI should be a teacher, not a replacement.

Children today will grow up alongside AI. The right introduction isn't through chatbots. It's through play. When a child sees how their drawing shapes the animation, they learn AI responds to their creative input. That's AI literacy.

🇪🇺

Built in Berlin, with European care.

This is a European product, built with European standards. No personal data is collected from your child, and AI processing only ever touches the drawing itself. Strong privacy isn't a feature here. It's the default.
