The story behind Storybook Adventures

Built in Berlin · Made for kids ages 6–12

Storybook Adventures was built around a simple belief: the best way to introduce a child to creativity, technology, and AI is by starting with the most familiar thing in the world — a piece of paper and a pencil.

From the founder

Hi, I’m Constantin. I built Storybook Adventures because I was tired of two things at the same time: kids spending hours scrolling through algorithm-curated content, and parents being afraid that AI would take creativity away from their children.

Both of those concerns are real. But I saw a third path. What if AI wasn’t the thing that made the art — just the thing that brought the child’s art to life? What if a creative app didn’t reward endless scrolling, but rewarded sitting at the kitchen table with a pencil?

That’s what we built. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Just real drawings, real children, and a little bit of magic at the end.

— Constantin Klöcker, Founder

What we believe

Privacy isn’t a feature, it’s the foundation.

Children never share their name, email, or any personal information. There’s no account to create. The app uses an anonymous device identifier — nothing more. We comply with GDPR and COPPA by design, not by retrofit.

Real creativity starts on real paper.

Most digital drawing apps replace pencil and paper. We built the opposite: an app where most of the creative time happens off-screen, on real paper, with real tools. The phone is just the camera and the cinema.

AI should be a teacher, not a replacement.

Children today will grow up alongside AI. We think the right way to introduce them isn’t through chatbots — it’s through play. When a child sees how their drawing shapes the animation, they’re learning the most important thing about AI: that it responds to their creative input. That’s the foundation of AI literacy.

Built in Berlin, with European care.

Our servers are in Frankfurt. Our company is in Germany. Our privacy standards are the strictest available. We think kids’ data deserves the same protection adults expect — and then some.

Where we’re going

Storybook Adventures launched on the Apple App Store in early 2026 with 24 story worlds for children ages 6–12. An Android version is in active development — you can join the waitlist on our homepage.

We’re also working on more story worlds, deeper character interactions, and ways for children to share their creations safely with family. If you want to follow along, the newsletter is the best way.
