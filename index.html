Common questions

Things parents ask first.

Is the app safe for my child?
Yes, and we built it that way from day one. There's no account, no email, no name, and no personal data of any kind collected from your child. No ads, no trackers, no social features, no chat. Every uploaded drawing is automatically screened by an AI safety check before it animates. Anything inappropriate is rejected, the token is refunded, and the image is deleted. Purchases always require an adult (see below). We comply with GDPR and COPPA by design.
What age is it for, and how does it work?
Designed for children ages 6 to 12, with support for younger kids alongside a parent. The flow is simple: (1) pick a story world from 24 adventures, (2) draw the scene on real paper with whatever you have: pencils, crayons, markers, paint, (3) photograph the drawing through the app, (4) watch the AI bring it to life as a short animated movie, (5) finish all three scenes to unlock the full storybook film. Most kids get hooked on the drawing part. The screen is just the camera and the cinema.
How does AI fit in, and is it good for kids?
The AI's only job is to bring your child's drawing to motion. It doesn't invent characters, write stories, or hold conversations. It responds to what they put on paper. That's deliberate: pediatric researchers and child-safety organizations consistently flag AI companions and open-ended chatbots as the patterns that aren't safe for under-13s. Bounded creative outputs like ours are recommended instead. As a side effect, kids naturally learn how AI works: their input shapes the output. That's the foundation of AI literacy, and a safer first AI experience than handing a child ChatGPT.
What about screen time?
Most of the creative time happens off-screen, on real paper. The phone is only used for a few seconds to photograph the drawing and a minute or two to watch the result. We call it focused screen time: more imagination, more drawing, less mindless scrolling. Many parents tell us their kids draw more after using the app, because they're curious to see how the AI will respond to their next picture.
What does it cost?
The app is free to download. Each animation uses one token, and tokens are sold in packs through Apple In-App Purchase. No subscriptions. Every purchase goes through two gates: our in-app parental gate (PIN or math challenge) plus Apple's standard sign-in (Face ID, Touch ID, or Apple ID password). Parents can also lock the shop entirely. All refunds are handled by Apple via reportaproblem.apple.com. EU consumers have additional withdrawal rights detailed in our Terms of Service.
Do I need internet? How fast is it?
Yes. Animating a scene runs on our servers, so an internet connection is required during processing. Drawing on paper, of course, does not. Each animation takes roughly two to three minutes from photo to finished video. A progress bar keeps kids in the loop. Once an animation is finished, it can be saved to the photo gallery and watched offline anytime.
Does it work on iPad? When is Android coming?
Yes. Storybook Adventures runs on both iPhone and iPad, with a layout adapted for the larger screen. An Android version is in active development. Sign up for the waitlist on the homepage and we'll send you a one-time email the moment it launches. No other emails, no spam.
Can I save and share the films? Where can I get help?
Completed animations can be saved to your device's photo gallery and shared from there, or kept entirely private. Your call. For anything else, email contact@storybookadventures.io and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days.
