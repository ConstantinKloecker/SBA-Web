2. What the App Does

Storybook Adventures is a creative app that transforms children's hand-drawn pictures into AI-powered animations:

  1. Choose a storyline: Select from guided story themes, each with three scenes and drawing prompts
  2. Draw on paper: Follow the scene description and draw on real paper
  3. Photograph and upload: Take a photo of the drawing through the App
  4. Watch the animation: AI processes the drawing and returns a short animated video

Each animation uses one token, purchased through Apple In-App Purchase or redeemed via a promotional voucher code. The App also offers a Custom Story mode where users create their own scenes with custom text prompts.

4. Anonymous Accounts

4.1 How Accounts Work

The App does not require registration. On first use, a random anonymous identifier (UUID) is generated and stored in the device's secure Keychain. This links the device to a server-side profile storing your token balance, drawings, animations, custom prompts, and scene progress.

This identifier is not connected to any name, email address, Apple ID, or personal information. For data requests, a support reference code derived from your identifier is available under Settings → My Data → Data Request.

4.2 Account Persistence and Recovery

Your account is tied to the anonymous identifier stored in the device Keychain:

  • App reinstall on the same device: Account, token balance, drawings, animations, and scene progress are restored automatically from our servers.
  • Factory reset with iCloud Keychain enabled: If iCloud Keychain is active and you restore from an iCloud backup or sign into the same iCloud account on a new device, the identifier is preserved and your account recovers automatically.
  • Factory reset without iCloud Keychain, or new Apple ID: The Keychain identifier is lost and cannot be recovered by us. Your server-side content (drawings, animations, scene progress) can no longer be accessed, as we have no way to identify your anonymous profile without the identifier. Token balance restoration may be possible via purchase records — see §5.5.
  • Multiple devices: Each device has its own independent account. Tokens and progress do not sync across devices unless the same iCloud Keychain identifier is present.

We are not liable for loss of account access resulting from factory resets, iCloud Keychain changes, new Apple IDs, or disabled iCloud Keychain.

4.3 Automatic Token Recovery

Token balance recovery is fully automatic and requires no user action. After reinstalling the App on the same device (or restoring via iCloud Keychain), your account is recovered using the anonymous identifier described in §4.2, and your token balance is restored from our servers. Any in-flight purchase that was charged by Apple but not yet credited to your balance is also recovered automatically the next time the App launches via Apple's StoreKit transaction listener. If your balance still appears incorrect after recovery, see §5.5.

6.3 Content Guidelines

Acceptable content:

  • Hand-drawn artwork on paper (pencil, crayon, marker, paint, etc.)
  • Creative scenes, characters, landscapes, objects, and stories
  • Any drawing style appropriate for the intended age group

Prohibited content — drawings and custom prompts must NOT contain:

  • Violence, weapons, or graphic imagery
  • Nudity or sexually suggestive content
  • Drug or alcohol references
  • Horror or disturbing imagery inappropriate for children
  • Personal information (names, addresses, phone numbers, school names)
  • Hate symbols, slurs, or discriminatory content
  • Real photographs, screenshots, or digitally generated images (these are not drawings and will be rejected)
  • Copyrighted characters, logos, or artwork belonging to others, uploaded without authorisation

6.4 Content Moderation

All uploaded drawings are automatically screened by OpenAI's content moderation system before animation. Drawings that violate our content guidelines are rejected. When a drawing is rejected:

  • The token is automatically refunded to your balance
  • For safety violations (violence, nudity, etc.): the image is immediately deleted from our servers
  • A digital fingerprint of the rejected image and an AI-generated description are stored for abuse monitoring — the image itself is not retained for safety violations
  • Repeat violations may result in service restrictions

6.5 Saving Animations

Completed animations can be saved to the device's photo gallery. Once saved, those files exist independently of the App. We recommend saving important animations to the gallery as an additional backup, as server-side copies may be removed if you delete your account data.
